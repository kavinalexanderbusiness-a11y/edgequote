Board 01 · The thesis

The next generation isn't more software.
It's fewer taps between the truck and the money.

EdgeQuote already has what the incumbents charge $300/month for: one ledger, one route engine, one capacity engine, an offline field day, explainable intelligence on every surface. What it doesn't have yet is compression — the audit found the right answer living two pages away from the question, on almost every screen. This direction keeps every engine and collapses the distance.

Fewer clicks

Every number is a tap to the filtered list it names. Every warning carries its own fix. The audited ledger on board 03 commits to targets — reschedule falls 5 → 2, portal-check falls 10 → 5.

Mobile first, glove first

A bottom tab bar ends the hamburger tax. Stage-primary actions render as one giant button. 44px targets everywhere a gloved thumb acts — the standard the codebase already wrote down, applied without exception.

AI that drafts, never decides

Drafts, explains, ranks, pre-fills. Never prices, never sends. The heuristic engines stay the deciders; the assistant turns their output into words and saves the typing.

Hierarchy = the one next action

Boards open on what to do, not what exists. Vanity counts die ("N jobs on the calendar"); stage-value tiles live ($1,215 awaiting approval → tap).

Premium is consistency

Syne + DM Sans, the ink/green token system, one motion vocabulary — already shipped, already good. The redesign extends the language; it never forks it.

Honest by construction

Unknown renders as "—", never 0. Empty books say so. Engine provenance rides every price. The product's honesty rules become visible design features.

Board 09 · Money

One ledger, told honestly —
down to the empty books.

Every figure here is an engine's own output — summarizeTransactions for cash, the aging read off invoiceBalance, margins from marginPct. The design's job is honesty made visible: unknown is "—" never zero, credit settlements never read as new money, and books with no expenses say so instead of boasting a 100% margin.

One chart, one hue, one point of emphasis. The week's cash as quiet bars; only the day that matters wears the accent, labeled directly. No axis theatre for five numbers.
The empty-books banner is the design. Margin renders "—" and the page says why — the honesty rule the P&L already enforces, promoted to a first-class visual state everywhere money shows.Rule: unknown cost ≠ zero cost · marginPct → null
Batch invoicing waits for its freeze. The action is drawn dead-center because the research says it earns its place (Jobber); the caption says it ships when 66de14f lifts. Mockups can be patient.

Board 11 · The assistant

Speak plainly. The engines execute.
Nothing fires without your yes.

The command palette is already the ONE palette — pages register verbs into it, and the module registry keeps it honest. It grows three things: recents (visit 1,000 shouldn't look like visit 1), natural-language actions parsed into existing engine calls behind the existing confirms, and a phone bottom-sheet so it stops being a desktop luxury.

Parse, plan, confirm — in that order. ServiceTitan's Atlas pattern, constrained the EdgeQuote way: the plan card names the engine, the capacity check, and the comms it will draft. The confirm is the same dialog hand-moves use.Research: ServiceTitan Atlas · seam: pageCommands
Recents make it a command surface on the phone too — bottom-sheet presentation, frecency list, and message results finally carry the ?c= deep link the inbox already supports.Audit: palette "message results navigate to the bare list"
Board 12 · What stays

A redesign you can trust is one that knows what not to touch.

This direction recomposes surfaces. It does not add engines, does not unfreeze lanes, and does not relitigate settled decisions. The constraints below aren't caveats — they're the reason the rest of the deck is buildable.

Frozen lanes, honored

Pricing (V2 roadmap only) · Customer Journey 7573dd9 · Messaging 2576b79 · Scheduling 1d4ef66 · Marketing+Photo 4627924 · Quotes/Invoices/Payments 66de14f. Boards touching them are drawn as post-freeze direction and labeled so.

Approved specs, rendered

Quote presentation = the Phase 6 spec's 17 decisions (board 07 is an illustration of it, not a rival — #17 forbids rivals). Booking is its own project and appears nowhere here. Sequencing law kept: observable → delivered → beautiful.

One engine per answer

invoiceBalance · needsFollowUp · lib/signals · capacityForDate · dayProfitability/gradeRoute · optimizeRoute/computeDayEtas · reach + applyConsent · buildTimeline · renderMessage · the ONE outbox · the ONE palette · the module registry. Every mockup consumes; none competes.

Field invariants

Offline bundle + outbox (complete = patch+invoice+text, one op) · day-view default · stage-primary progression · verified undo · the recurring scope choice. The redesign renders them bigger, never thinner.

Honesty rules

Unknown ≠ zero, everywhere. Empty books announce themselves. Season-to-date compares like with like. "Sent" is one writer's word. A derived state is never stored. These are design features now, not footnotes.

Recorded non-bugs

The annualizer claim is false — don't chase it. Charge-route divergence is verified harmless. Quote expiry stays display-only. MOW_LABEL waits for its own project. The deck inherits the project's memory, including its retractions.
